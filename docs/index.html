JLC can finish the electronics

The production route is now Standard PCBA on both sides, with specialty parts shipped directly into JLC's consigned-parts inventory. JLC manually installs the switches and encoder, fits the joystick, flashes the STM32, runs the supplied USB control test, and installs caps on the PRIMARY unit. The customer only closes the CNC enclosure with four screws after first-article fit approval.
