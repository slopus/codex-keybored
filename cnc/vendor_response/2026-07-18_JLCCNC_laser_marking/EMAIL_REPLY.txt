This confirmation applies only to:
- Part: CM2-002_bottom_weight.step
- CNC machining item: CNC2607185001881-3086316A

Manufacturing instructions:
1. Please suppress/ignore the recessed text geometry in the STEP model. Do not CNC-machine the 0.25 mm-deep text pockets.
2. Complete the fine bead blast and black matte anodizing first, then laser mark the desk-facing underside.
3. The intended result is a natural-aluminum/silver mark on the black anodized surface.
4. Use CM2-002_LASER_ARTWORK_ONLY_RevB.dxf as the production artwork. It is 1:1 in millimeters, with the origin at the part center. All lettering is converted to closed vector outlines; there are no font, TEXT, or MTEXT entities.
5. Hatch/fill the LASER_MARK contours as compound glyph boundaries and preserve the enclosed counters/holes in letters such as O, D, A, B, and R. Do not render only a hairline stroke.
6. Use CM2-002_LASER_PLACEMENT_REFERENCE_RevB.dxf and the PDF approval sheet for alignment only. Only the LASER_MARK layer is production artwork. Do not laser any layer whose name ends in DO_NOT_MARK.
7. View and mark the UNDERSIDE / DESK-FACING SURFACE. +Y points toward the rear, thick edge, and USB side. Do not resize, rotate, mirror, or substitute the artwork.
8. Preserve every other feature in the STEP model, including the G-65 O-ring groove, four mounting holes/counterbores, wedge angle, and external dimensions.
